FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Click on the "Register" button in the navigation bar. Fill in your details including your roll number, department, and semester. After registration, you can log in to access all features.

Once logged in, you'll see a bookmark icon on each notice. Click it to save the notice. You can view all your saved notices in your profile page under the "Saved Notices" section.

On each notice detail page, you'll find share buttons for various platforms. You can also copy the direct link to the notice and share it via email or messaging apps.

Check your notification settings in your profile. Ensure your email is correct. You can also contact the administrator through our contact form for assistance.
